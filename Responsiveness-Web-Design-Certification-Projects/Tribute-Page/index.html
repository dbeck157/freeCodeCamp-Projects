Reasons to visit Reykjavic, Iceland

Reykjavic was founded in 1786 as an official trading town. It has since grown into what is now that largest capital city of Iceland. With a population of just over 120,000, it has become a hub for tourists around the world. Here are a few reasons for which one would travel to Reykjavik:

The Blue Lagoon

These milky-blue, geothermal waters are on everyones 'Top 10' lists. Just a 20 minute ride from the city, it is a must-visit.

Hallgrimskirkja Church

Towering above the city of Reykjavic is Hallgrimskirkja Church. This monument to architecture can be seen from nearly every corner of the city. From atop the church, you'll find the view breathtaking.

Art & Culture

With wonderful mueums and art galleries, there are plenty of works of art to take in. Street art is also scattered across the city. You may just stumble upon a few masterpieces.

If you would like to learn more about Reykjavic and the wonders it holds, please visit their city website.
